Together, Let's Light Up the Sky

About Us

We are a passionate group of high school students dedicated to empowering underprivileged youth through theatre workshops. We noticed that in India, performing arts are a highly stigmatized field and are viewed as a mere hobby. Through our collective experience in the performing arts we aim to break this notion of the art sector. Our mission is to foster creativity, confidence, and communication skills in underprivileged children- helping them express themselves and discover their hidden talents. Let's help our community, one workshop at a time!
